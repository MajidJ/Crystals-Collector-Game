Crystals Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random Target Number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to Your Number.

You win the game by matching Your Number to the Target Number, you lose the game if Your Number goes above the Target Number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
